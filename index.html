About Me

Designing experiences that matter

I design enterprise products that work — for the people using them and the businesses building them. 15+ years of progressive experience leading UX across JPMorganChase, Salesforce, Fidelity, and Cognizant.

As an HFI Certified Usability Analyst (CUA) — fewer than 10,000 active holders globally — I've led end-to-end design and research across 30+ enterprise client engagements in fintech, banking, enterprise SaaS, healthcare, and e-commerce. I run mixed-methods research programs, shape product strategy at the C-suite level, and build design systems that scale.

I actively use AI-assisted workflows — Claude AI, Figma AI, and Microsoft Copilot — to accelerate delivery and focus design effort on the highest-impact decisions. Effective as a senior individual contributor, UX research lead, and team mentor.
