About Me

Designing experiences that matter

I design mission-critical enterprise products where decisions carry real business and regulatory consequence. I've spent my career leading end-to-end product design across fintech, banking, and regulated enterprise SaaS at JPMorganChase, Salesforce, Fidelity, and Cognizant.

As an HFI Certified Usability Analyst (CUA) — fewer than 10,000 active holders globally — I specialize in high-density, mission-critical interfaces for product, risk, and compliance stakeholders. Proven pattern of turning ambiguous, cross-functional problems into shipped, measurable outcomes: cycle-time reductions, adoption lifts, and data-quality gains on platforms used by enterprise customers, sales teams, and internal operators.

I actively use AI-assisted workflows — Claude AI, Figma AI, and Microsoft Copilot — both to accelerate research synthesis and to design AI-native product experiences. Now targeting Staff / Principal-level scope: setting UX direction across a product area, building design systems that scale, and mentoring designers around research-led craft.
